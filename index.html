ABOUT THE EVENT

Vidyut'25 stands as a premier showcase of innovation and sustainable mobility, bringing the future of transportation into the present. Hosted at the prestigious Maulana Azad National Institute of Technology (MANIT), Bhopal, this event serves as a high-octane intersection where technology meets environmental consciousness. From the expansive EV Expo, featuring a diverse fleet of next-gen electric vehicles and cutting-edge charging solutions, to the intellectually stimulating Tech Track 2.0, the event is designed to spark a revolution in how we move. Whether you are an industry professional, a tech enthusiast, or a student, Vidyut'25 offers a unique platform to witness firsthand the power of clean energy and the silent, high-performance engineering that is redefining the global automotive landscape.

The Future is Electric: EVOLVE Club x MANIT Bhopal Get ready to witness the spark of innovation as MANIT Bhopal takes center stage in the electric revolution!
